Company Name: DemoShop GmbH
Industry: E-commerce and Retail
Headquarters: Berlin, Germany
Founded: 2019
Employees: ~120
Markets Served: Germany, Austria, Switzerland, Netherlands, France
Business Model: Direct-to-consumer online retail

Company Overview:
DemoShop GmbH is a European e-commerce company specializing in high-quality
consumer goods sold directly through its online platform. The company focuses
on reliable delivery, transparent policies, and customer-centric support.
Orders are fulfilled from regional warehouses in Germany and the Netherlands.

Product Categories:
- Home and Living
- Electronics Accessories
- Lifestyle and Personal Care
- Office and Work Essentials

Sales Channels:
- Official website (primary)
- Mobile web
- No third-party marketplaces (Amazon, eBay not used)

Customer Support Philosophy:
DemoShop aims to resolve customer requests efficiently and transparently.
Automated support handles common questions, while complex or sensitive cases
are escalated to human agents.

Order Processing:
- Orders are processed within 24 hours on business days
- Customers receive a confirmation email after placing an order
- Orders cannot be modified once shipped

Shipping:
- Germany and Austria: 2 to 5 business days
- EU international: 5 to 10 business days
- Shipping partners: DHL, DPD
- Tracking number is sent by email after shipment

Order Tracking:
- Customers must provide an order number (format: DS-XXXXXX)
- Tracking information becomes available within 24 hours after shipment

Returns and Refunds:
- Return window: 30 days after delivery
- Products must be unused and in original packaging
- Proof of purchase (order number or invoice) required
- Refunds are processed within 5 to 10 business days
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method

Cancellations:
- Orders can be cancelled only before shipment
- Once shipped, customers must follow the return process

Payments and Security:
- Supported payments: credit card, PayPal, Klarna
- Customer support never asks for:
  - credit card numbers
  - passwords
  - verification codes

Account and Privacy:
- Password resets must be done via the official website
- Support agents cannot change passwords
- Customer data is handled according to GDPR

Complaints and Escalation:
- Angry or frustrated customers should be acknowledged calmly
- If the issue cannot be resolved automatically, escalate to human support
- Human support response time: within 24 to 48 hours
