FAQ - DemoShop GmbH

Orders and shipping
1) How long does delivery take?
   - Germany and Austria: 2 to 5 business days.
   - EU international: 5 to 10 business days.

2) When will I receive a tracking number?
   - A tracking number is sent by email after shipment.
   - Tracking usually becomes active within 24 hours.

3) Can I change my delivery address?
   - Only before shipment. Once shipped, the address cannot be modified.

4) Can I cancel my order?
   - Only before shipment. If already shipped, use the return process.

Returns and refunds
5) What is the return window?
   - 30 days after delivery. Items must be unused and in original packaging.

6) How do refunds work?
   - Proof of purchase is required.
   - Refunds are processed within 5 to 10 business days to the original payment method.

7) Do you offer exchanges?
   - Exchanges are allowed within 30 days for unused items.
   - Customer pays return shipping unless the item is defective.

Payments and security
8) What payment methods are supported?
   - Credit card, PayPal, Klarna.

9) Do you store payment details?
   - No. DemoShop does not store credit card numbers or verification codes.

Account and privacy
10) How do I reset my password?
   - Use the official website password reset flow. Support cannot reset passwords.

11) How is my data handled?
   - Data is handled according to GDPR and used only to fulfill orders and support requests.
   - Customers can request data export or deletion via support.

Warranty and support
12) What warranty is offered?
   - 1-year limited warranty on small appliances for manufacturing defects only.

13) How do I contact support?
   - Email support@demoshop.example or use in-app chat.
   - Human support responds within 24 to 48 hours.
